Limestone Powder Overview

Limestone powder, also known as calcium carbonate (CaCO₃), is a versatile mineral powder with extensive applications across multiple industries. Our premium limestone powder is processed from high-quality limestone deposits to meet the specific requirements of construction, agriculture, water treatment, and industrial applications.

Key Properties

  • Chemical Formula: CaCO₃ (Calcium Carbonate)
  • Purity: 95-98% CaCO₃
  • Whiteness: 85-95%
  • pH: 8.5-9.5
  • Moisture: <0.5%
  • Oil Absorption: 15-25 g/100g

Applications

Construction

Used in cement production, concrete mixing, and soil stabilization for enhanced strength and durability.

Agriculture

Essential for soil pH adjustment, providing calcium for plant growth and improving soil structure.

Water Treatment

Used for pH adjustment, hardness removal, and impurity neutralization in water treatment processes.

Industrial

Applications in glass manufacturing, steel production, flue gas desulfurization, and chemical processes.
