Dolomite Powder

Dolomite Powder is a double carbonate of calcium and magnesium with a unique crystalline structure. It is used as a filler in paints, plastics, rubber, and ceramics, and is valued for its high purity, whiteness, and ability to improve product strength and durability.

Applications

  • Paints and coatings
  • Plastic and polymer industry
  • Rubber industry
  • Ceramics and glass
  • Construction materials

Properties

  • High purity and whiteness
  • Good dispersibility
  • Improves mechanical properties
  • Low moisture content
  • Stable chemical composition
