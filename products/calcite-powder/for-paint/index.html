Calcite Powder for Paint

Our 400–1500 mesh calcite powder delivers maximum brightness and opacity for premium paint formulations. With ≤25 g/100g oil absorption and ≥95% whiteness, it ensures smooth flow, excellent coverage, and superior finish quality.

Benefits in Paints & Coatings

  • Maximum brightness and whiteness (≥95%)
  • Low oil absorption for better paint flow
  • Superior opacity and hiding power
  • Excellent dispersibility and stability
  • Cost-effective filler for premium formulations

Recommended Grades for Paint Applications

  • 400–600 mesh (15–25 microns): Interior emulsions, standard coatings
  • 600–800 mesh (8–15 microns): Premium interior paints, automotive primers
  • 800–1500 mesh (2–15 microns): High-end coatings, automotive finishes
