Calcite Powder

Calcite Powder is a natural form of calcium carbonate with extremely high whiteness, purity, and free-flowing in nature. It is widely used in plastics, paints, rubber, and paper industries due to its low oil absorption, high dispersibility, and compatibility with polymers and other organic additives.

Applications

  • Plastic and polymer industry (as a filler)
  • Paints and coatings
  • Rubber industry
  • Paper manufacturing
  • Ceramics and glass

Properties

  • High purity and brightness
  • Low oil absorption
  • Excellent dispersibility
  • Stable chemical properties
  • Fine particle size
