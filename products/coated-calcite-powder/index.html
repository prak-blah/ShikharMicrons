What is Coated Calcite Powder?

Coated Calcite Powder is surface-treated with stearic acid or other agents to enhance its compatibility with polymers and improve dispersion. It is widely recognized for its exceptional performance in high-end applications, superior processability, and enhanced mechanical properties. Due to its unique characteristics, industries worldwide use this premium powder as a functional filler in demanding polymer applications.

Uses of Coated Calcite Powder in Various Industries

Our coated calcite powder serves as a premium filler and functional additive across multiple industries:

Plastic and Polymer Industry

As a functional filler in high-performance plastics, coated calcite powder improves mechanical properties, reduces material costs, and enhances dimensional stability. It's widely used in automotive parts, electrical components, and premium plastic products.

Cable and Wire Manufacturing

In cable and wire manufacturing, coated calcite powder acts as a filler that improves insulation properties, enhances mechanical strength, and provides excellent electrical performance while maintaining cost efficiency.

Rubber Industry

Rubber manufacturers use coated calcite powder to improve processing characteristics, enhance mechanical properties, and reduce costs in high-performance rubber products, automotive components, and industrial applications.

Paint and Coating Industry

In premium paints and coatings, coated calcite powder acts as an extender pigment that improves opacity, reduces costs, and enhances durability while providing superior dispersion and application properties.
