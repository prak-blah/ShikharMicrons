Coated Calcite Powder

Coated Calcite Powder is surface-treated with stearic acid or other agents to enhance its compatibility with polymers and improve dispersion. It is widely used in the plastic, rubber, and cable industries for its superior performance and processability.

Applications

  • Plastic and polymer industry (as a functional filler)
  • Cable and wire manufacturing
  • Rubber industry
  • Paints and coatings

Properties

  • Improved compatibility with polymers
  • Excellent dispersion
  • Hydrophobic surface
  • High brightness and purity
  • Low oil absorption
