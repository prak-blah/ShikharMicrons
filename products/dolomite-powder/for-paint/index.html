Dolomite Powder for Paint

Our 300–600 mesh dolomite powder is optimized for opacity, flow, and scrub resistance in architectural and industrial coatings. With consistent PSD and controlled moisture, it integrates seamlessly into water-based and solvent-based systems.

Benefits in Paints & Coatings

  • Improves opacity and hiding power
  • Enhances scrub and stain resistance
  • Optimizes rheology for smooth application
  • Reduces formulation cost while maintaining performance

Recommended Grades

  • 300–400 mesh: Exterior emulsions, durability focus
  • 400–600 mesh: Interior emulsions, premium finish

Typical Specifications

Whiteness≥ 92%
Oil Absorption≤ 28 g/100g
Moisture≤ 0.3%
