What is Coated Dolomite Powder?

Coated Dolomite Powder is surface-modified to improve its compatibility with polymers and enhance its performance in advanced industrial applications. It is widely recognized for its exceptional strength-enhancing properties, superior processability, and enhanced mechanical performance. Due to its unique characteristics, industries worldwide use this premium powder as a functional filler in demanding polymer applications.

Uses of Coated Dolomite Powder in Various Industries

Our coated dolomite powder serves as a premium filler and functional additive across multiple industries:

Plastic and Polymer Industry

As a functional filler in high-performance plastics, coated dolomite powder improves mechanical properties, reduces material costs, and enhances dimensional stability. It's widely used in automotive parts, electrical components, and premium plastic products.

Rubber Industry

Rubber manufacturers use coated dolomite powder to improve processing characteristics, enhance mechanical properties, and reduce costs in high-performance rubber products, automotive components, and industrial applications.

Paint and Coating Industry

In premium paints and coatings, coated dolomite powder acts as an extender pigment that improves opacity, reduces costs, and enhances durability while providing superior dispersion and application properties.

Adhesives and Sealants

In adhesives and sealants, coated dolomite powder improves rheological properties, enhances bonding strength, and provides cost-effective filler solutions for various applications.
