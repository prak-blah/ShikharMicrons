Coated Dolomite Powder

Coated Dolomite Powder is surface-modified to improve its compatibility with polymers and enhance its performance in advanced industrial applications. It is widely used in plastics, rubber, and paints for its improved dispersion and hydrophobic properties.

Applications

  • Plastic and polymer industry
  • Rubber industry
  • Paints and coatings
  • Adhesives and sealants

Properties

  • Hydrophobic surface
  • Improved dispersion in polymers
  • High brightness and purity
  • Low oil absorption
  • Stable chemical composition
