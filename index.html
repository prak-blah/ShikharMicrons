About Shikhar Microns

We are a leading manufacturer of micronized calcium carbonate (calcite) and dolomite powder, serving key industries such as paints, plastics, rubber, detergents, and polymers. Backed by advanced grinding technology, an automated powder coating unit, and a fully equipped lab, we ensure consistent particle size and top-tier product quality tailored to industrial needs.

Founded by Mr. Tilak Jain, who brings over 27 years of expertise in mineral grinding and quality control, our company has grown into a trusted supplier across India. His leadership and commitment to excellence have helped us build a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction in the mineral processing industry.
