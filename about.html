Who We Are

Premier manufacturer of micronized calcium carbonate and dolomite powder, serving India's leading industries with quality and trust since 1997.

Our Story

We are a premier manufacturer of micronized calcium carbonate (calcite) and dolomite powder, serving industries such as paints, plastics, rubber, detergents, and polymers. With multiple factories across India, we operate advanced production units equipped with Raymond mills, ball mills, vertical roller mills, and fully automatic powder coating machines. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and laboratories ensure consistent quality and reliable supply.

Founded by Mr. Tilak Jain in 1997, our company draws on over 27 years of expertise in mineral grinding and quality control. His leadership has shaped our high standards and customer-focused approach.

Today, we are recognized as a trusted supplier of high-quality mineral powders, known for our multi-location presence, technological diversity, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
